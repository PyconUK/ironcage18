Children’s tickets are £6 per child and include a sandwich lunch.

Children will need to be accompanied by a responsible adult, who will remain at the event during the day.

Lunch will be provided for accompanying adults, there is no extra charge for an adult’s lunch.

If you need to bring younger children with you in order to be able to accompany a child, please do, but let us know.

Please note that only one ticket can be purchased at a time, but multiple orders can be placed.

All prices include VAT at 20%.
