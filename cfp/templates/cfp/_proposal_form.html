Ticket information

At PyCon UK, all attendees - including speakers and even the conference organising committee - need to have a ticket. We don't automatically issue speakers with free tickets, or provide benefits such as accommodation or travel funds.

However, we are aware that some speakers need to pay their own way, and not all will find it easy to meet the various costs of attending a conference.

Every speaker who needs a free ticket will receive one. However, you must request it through our financial assistance programme. Speakers who feel they can afford only a portion of the price of a ticket are welcome to select purchase theirs at the lower Unwaged rate if that is a suitable option for them.

In addition, if you require assistance in the form of funds for travel, accommodation or other expenses, we warmly invite you to apply for those through the financial assistance programme. We will do all we can to make it possible for you to speak at our conference.

Please do this as early as possible - preferably right now.

We request that if you can readily afford it, or if your employer would otherwise support your attendance by purchasing a ticket on your behalf, you don't claim a free ticket. We rely on our attendees and speakers to make their own judgements about this.

In short, if as a speaker you need the help of a free ticket or other financial assistance, we'd like to make sure you get it.

We believe this is the fairest policy, and allows us to offer the most financial assistance to attendees who need it.
